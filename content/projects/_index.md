## Projects

--- 


### Systems

* [cvc5](https://cvc5.github.io), an industrial strengh SMT solver

* [Kind 2](http://kind2-mc.github.io/kind2/), a multi-engine model checker for infinite-state systems

* [SMT-LIB](http://www.SMT-LIB.org/), a standard I/O language for SMT solvers

* [MoXI](https://www.aere.iastate.edu/modelchecker/), an intermediate language for model checking

* [SMTCoq](https://smtcoq.github.io), a trustworthy integration of SMT solvers into the Coq proof assistant

* [VERDICT](https://ge-high-assurance.github.io/VERDICT), an architectural and behavioral analyzer of AADL models
